Cheese Lasagna

Description

Cheese lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of lasagna noodles, cheese, and tomato sauce.
It is a comforting and delicious meal that is popular all over the world.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mozzarella Chesse
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 lb lasagna noodles

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  • Brown the ground beef and drain the fat.
  • Add the spaghetti sauce to the ground beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Assemble the lasagna by layering the ingredients in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  • Bake the lasagna for 30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
